Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main function of the app?
CyberBeads is a digital mala beads app designed for Apple Watch. It simulates the tactile feedback of counting beads to help users find moments of peace and focus in their busy daily lives.
Does the app collect my personal data?
No. CyberBeads does not collect, store, or process any user personal information or usage data. All features run locally on your device.
Does the app require an internet connection?
No. CyberBeads operates completely offline and does not require any internet connection.
Which Apple Watch models are supported?
CyberBeads supports all Apple Watch models running watchOS 7.0 and above.
